TROPHYKNIFE EP LAUNCH

December 30 2011 will see local metal boys TROPHYKNIFE share the stage with their hardcore pals in Bear Witness, Trainwreck and Our Solace, in belated recognition of the release of TK’s second independent EP “Trapped in the Gradge”, and also to celebrate the apocalyptic chaos the ending of the world, as predicted; 2012 will bring us.

LOCATION & TIME

At the Bendigo hotel from 8pm is where you’ll be able to catch the assault these 4 Melbourne based acts will deliver, as well as enjoy the ending of the world with good friends, good times and of course, many, many beers.
